Nexus Mesh

AI-Coordinated Healthcare Trust Mesh & Life-Continuity System A decentralized, compliance-aligned coordination fabric for patient-centered care, organizational sovereignty, and AI-assisted medical workflows.

Overview

Nexus Mesh is a next-generation healthcare coordination framework designed to:

Maintain patient autonomy and control over consent

Preserve health system sovereignty (no forced data centralization)

Enable AI-augmented clinical decision flow

Improve care continuity across facilities & life transitions

Provide a tamper-evident audit trail and compliance guardrails

Support federated learning without sharing raw PHI

Enable Observer AI to detect and flag safety/quality drift

Unlike traditional platforms, Nexus Mesh does not replace EHRs or force data integration. It creates a mesh trust layer to coordinate care, information, and policy enforcement across institutions ‚Äî without breaking privacy, compliance, or ownership boundaries.
